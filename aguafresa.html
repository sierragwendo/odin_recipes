Agua de Fresa

Delicious summer drink

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Lime
  • Mint sprigs
  1. Mix strawberries, sugar, and 1 cup water in medium bowl. Cover & refrigerate for 4 hours.
  2. Pour chilled mixture into blender and blend on high until smooth. Pour through strainer. Stir remaining water into mixture.
  3. Fill glass with ice. Serve juice. Garnish with mint.
