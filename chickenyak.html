Chicken Yakitori

Juicy chicken yakitori, a Japanese dish.

Ingredients

  • Wooden skewers
  • Chicken thighs
  • Scallions
  • Sake
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  1. Soak skewers in water for 15 min.
  2. Thread cut chicken pieces onto skewers.
  3. Combine sake, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar in a saucepan and boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 min. Reserve half the sauce for dipping.
  4. Heat a grill pan on high and brush with oil. Add skewers and cook chicken thoroughly. Baste with sauce until cooked.
