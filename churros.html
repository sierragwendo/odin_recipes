Churros

These churros are soft yet crispy.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Water
  • Margarine
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  1. Heat oil to 360 degrees
  2. Heat water and margarine Combine flour and salt Reduce heat and stir until ball forms. Remove from heat and beat in eggs. Spoon mixture in pastry bag with large tip.
  3. Squeeze 4 inch long strips of dough into oil Dont overcrowd. Fry for 2 min or until golden brown. Stir sugar and cinmmona, then roll Churros in mixture when still hot.
